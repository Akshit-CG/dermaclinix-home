HOW WE MEASURE SUCCESS

Your experience, measured honestly.

At DermaClinix, the question we ask is not just "did the procedure work?" but "do you feel better, inside and out?" We use globally recognised wellbeing and quality-of-life assessment frameworks to measure real outcomes — not just clinical appearances.

HOW WE TRACK YOUR WELLBEING
DLQI

Dermatology Life Quality Index

A simple questionnaire that asks how your skin or hair concerns have affected your daily life, work, relationships, and emotional wellbeing. Scored 0–30. We re-score below baseline before and after your procedure to measure real-life improvement.

WHO-5

World Health Organization Wellbeing Index

A 5-question survey developed by the WHO to measure overall psychological wellbeing — including mood, energy, and calmness. Scored 0–100. We use it as your personal happiness index, recorded before and at every follow-up.

HADS

Hospital Anxiety and Depression Scale

A 14-item screening tool used to understand any anxiety or emotional concerns you may be carrying before your procedure. Helps us understand your emotional starting point and ensure we support you the right way throughout your journey.

GAIS

Global Aesthetic Improvement Scale

An internationally used rating scale where, both the doctor and the guest, independently score how much aesthetic improvement has occurred. We compare both scores to make sure what we see matches how you feel — and flag any gaps early.

Which is the best hair transplant clinic in Delhi?

DermaClinix, every procedure begins with scalp analysis, donor assessment, and a long-term plan. Doctors — not technicians — perform graft extraction and implantation, which protects follicle survival and natural direction. Hygiene meets surgical benchmarks, and follow-ups are structured to support healing across twelve months.

How much does a hair transplant cost?

Cost depends on various factors including the extent of hair loss and the number of grafts required. We provide a complete, transparent estimate during your initial consultation.

Is the procedure safe?

Yes, when performed by certified dermatologists, hair transplants are very safe. We maintain strict hygiene protocols and surgical benchmarks to ensure the highest safety standards.

Is a hair transplant permanent?

Yes, transplanted hair follicles are typically resistant to the hormone that causes hair loss, meaning the results are designed to last a lifetime.

Which method gives the best results?

The best method depends on your unique hair pattern and donor area. Our doctors will analyze your scalp and recommend the most effective procedure for natural, lasting results.
